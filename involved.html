GET INVOLVED!

As part of its mission, CMTC provides a range of research, collaboration and employment opportunities. It is particularly strongly committed to fuller involvement of younger scholars from Oxford and other institutions worldwide.

BECOME PART OF THE COMMUNITY

If you share our passion for comparative research of manuscript and epigraphic traditions from different parts of the world, we encourage you to get involved in CMTC’s activities. There are many ways in which you can participate. For the beginning, you could present your research at one of our colloquia and get familiar with the members of our friendly team. If you have already participated in our events, it would be amazing to diversify the range of the Centre’s activities following your ideas! Finally, if you have a more ambitious project, we will be pleased to consider review your application for a workshop or a conference that we can assist you to organize.

JOB AND SCHOLARSHIP OPPORTUNITIES

CMTC has secured funding for a DPhil scholarship and a Junior Research Fellow position, and we accept applications for these positions roughly once in three years. Although no applications are accepted at this moment, you are encouraged to review the profiles of current and past recipients of CMTC scholarships and Junior Research Fellows. If you are a young schoalrs yourself, we would be delighted to receive your application when we open the next call for the graduate scholarship and JRF!
